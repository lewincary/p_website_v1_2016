LEWIN CARY

Bio.

Australian dev from Sydney living and studying in Palo Alto, California. Addict of dubstep, deep and anything bass - been producing experimental electronic music for the last 6 years, most recently under the Mary Droppins Project. Been tinkering with computers and synthesizers for as long as I can remember.

Education.

Studying computer science at Stanford University, California, concentrating in artificial intelligence. When I'm not lost somewhere in the stack you'll find me making noises up at CCRMA. Studied for a short while at the Australian National University. Old boy from Cranbrook School, Sydney.
